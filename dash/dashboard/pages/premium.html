Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ProMcBot Premium

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum). All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account dashboard. Changes take effect immediately, and you'll receive a prorated credit for the unused portion of your current plan.
Is there a free trial for Premium features?
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial for all premium plans. No credit card required to start your trial. You can cancel anytime during the trial period without being charged.
How many servers can I connect with one subscription?
The Pro Tier allows you to connect up to 10 servers. The Ultimate Tier offers unlimited server connections. All servers must be owned or administered by you.
