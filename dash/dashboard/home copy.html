Frequently Asked Questions

What is ProMcBot?
ProMcBot is a Discord bot that links Discord servers with Minecraft servers for effortless control.
How do I connect my server?
Invite ProMcBot to Discord, run the /link command, and follow the prompts to authorize your Minecraft server.
Is it free?
Yes—ProMcBot’s core linking functionality is completely free, with optional premium addons coming soon.
